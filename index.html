When I help

Bring me in when the challenge matters, crosses teams and needs senior attention.

I am most useful when the issue is too important to drift, too cross-functional to sit neatly in one department or too sensitive for a generic programme.

An important priority crosses several departments and nobody owns the whole picture.

The executive team needs outside thinking and practical support, not another long report.

The business has outgrown the way leaders, managers or teams currently work.

A strategy, brand, launch or change initiative is not landing through the organisation.

Managers need to step up, take greater ownership and lead with more confidence.

The CEO, MD or executive sponsor is carrying too much of the thinking and follow-through.

I help get the right people clear, capable and moving around the same priority.
